welcome to district science center, dharampur


District Science center, Dharampur, Valsad, GujaratDistrict Science center is located on Garden Road, Dharampur in Valsad District, Gujarat. District Science center was opened for the public on 27th April 1984.It houses several kinds of objects based on general sciences.


District Science center has also a planetarium and a Science park. District Science center , Dharmapur organizes mobile science exhibition to provide basic knowledge of science to the common people of the locality. It also organizes Science demonstration lecture and Science film shows. Apart from that the museum arranges several other activities in the locality.
